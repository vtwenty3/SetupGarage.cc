About SG

Setup Garage is web-platform for uploading and downloading car setups for the sim-racing title Asetto Corsa Competizione. It's target audience is sim-racing enthusiasts and people who want to learn more about race craft, car-mechanics and car setups. Check our Guides page where you can learn more about it!

About Me

I have been passonate about Tech and Racing since my earliest memories. This led me to study "Computing" in Edinburgh Napier University. As far as racing goes it is expensive and time consuming sport, especially for a student abroad, so this is where Sim-racing came as a safer and more-affordable alternative. The blend of these two passions resulted in this small project fully developed by me.
